Our Services



Decks & Porches

Enjoy entertaining outside? A deck can provide you with an amazing outdoor space. We can provide patio furniture for you, so that you have a comfortable area for people to interact and communicate, gather and eat.

Kitchen Remodel

Your kitchen can be renovated or remodeled by us. For example, if you require an open-plan kitchen that is linked to the dining room we can do it for you, so that you can cook while having a quality time with your family as well as entertaining guests.

Bedroom Remodel

Bedrooms are more about paint, fabrics, window treatments, flooring, lighting, wallpaper, and other lower-cost, DIY-friendly materials. Another great thing is that bedroom remodels can be a positive return on your investment.

Bathroom Remodel

This typically entails a new design layout, relocation of existing fixtures, or the addition of substantial new amenities such as a whirlpool, sauna, steam room, walk-in shower, windows other than replacements, or skylights.

Windows Application

Windows are shown on the floor plan by three parallel lines drawn within the walls. The window size or a number may be labeled on the window. A number will relate to a window schedule number.

Garage Remodel

Garage Living has completed thousands of garage renovation projects of varying scope and size. Our clients are always amazed at how quickly we can transform a cluttered or unfinished garage into a clean, organized space.
